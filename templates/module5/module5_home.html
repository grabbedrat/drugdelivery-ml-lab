Module 5: Toxicity Prediction and Adverse Effect Analysis

In this module, you will learn about predicting toxicity and analyzing adverse effects of drugs. The module covers the following topics:

  • Lesson 1: Toxicity Databases and Data Curation
  • Lesson 2: QSAR Models for Toxicity Prediction
  • Lesson 3: Adverse Event Mining and Analysis
  • Case Study: Assessing the Toxicity Risk of a Drug Candidate

Learning Objectives

By the end of this module, you will be able to:

  • Understand and curate toxicity databases.
  • Develop and apply QSAR models for toxicity prediction.
  • Perform adverse event mining and analysis.
  • Assess the toxicity risk of a drug candidate through a case study.
