Module 4: Drug Repurposing and Combination Therapy

In this module, you will learn about drug repurposing and combination therapy strategies. The module covers the following topics:

  • Lesson 1: Network-Based Approaches for Repurposing
  • Lesson 2: Signature-Based Methods and Transcriptomics
  • Lesson 3: Synergistic Drug Combinations
  • Project: Repurposing Approved Drugs for a New Indication

Learning Objectives

By the end of this module, you will be able to:

  • Understand and apply network-based approaches for drug repurposing.
  • Understand signature-based methods and transcriptomics for drug repurposing.
  • Analyze and identify synergistic drug combinations.
  • Complete a project on repurposing approved drugs for a new indication.
