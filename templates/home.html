Computational Drug Discovery and Delivery

Unlock the power of computational techniques to revolutionize drug development.

Course Overview

This comprehensive course delves into the cutting-edge computational methods and techniques used in the drug discovery and delivery process, from identifying potential drug candidates to optimizing their formulation and delivery.

Through a combination of theoretical concepts, practical examples, and hands-on projects, you'll gain a deep understanding of the computational tools and strategies employed by pharmaceutical researchers and drug developers.

Modules

The course is divided into eight modules, each covering a specific aspect of computational drug discovery and delivery:

  1. Foundations of Drug Discovery
  2. Machine Learning for Drug Property Prediction
  3. Structure-Based Drug Design
  4. Ligand-Based Drug Design
  5. Drug Repurposing and Combination Therapy
  6. Toxicity Prediction and Risk Assessment
  7. Drug Delivery and Formulation
  8. Cheminformatics Tools and Techniques
