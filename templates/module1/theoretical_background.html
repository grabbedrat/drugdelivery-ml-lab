Theoretical Background

Solubility and Its Significance

Solubility refers to the ability of a substance to dissolve in a solvent, typically water in the context of drug development. The solubility of a drug molecule plays a crucial role in its absorption, distribution, and overall bioavailability.

Machine Learning for Drug Property Prediction

Machine learning techniques have emerged as powerful tools for predicting various drug properties, including solubility. By leveraging historical data and molecular descriptors, machine learning models can learn patterns and relationships between chemical structures and their corresponding properties.

Molecular Descriptors

Molecular descriptors are numerical representations of chemical structures that capture various physicochemical properties. They serve as the input features for machine learning models, allowing them to learn the relationship between structure and property.
