Module 1: Fundamentals of Computational Drug Discovery

In this module, you will learn the fundamental concepts and techniques used in computational drug discovery. The module covers the following topics:

  • Lesson 1: Molecular Representation and Descriptors
  • Lesson 2: Data Preprocessing and Feature Engineering
  • Lesson 3: Machine Learning Basics for Drug Discovery

Learning Objectives

By the end of this module, you will be able to:

  • Understand various molecular representation methods and descriptors used in drug discovery.
  • Preprocess and engineer features from drug-related data.
  • Understand the basic concepts of machine learning and their applications in drug discovery.
