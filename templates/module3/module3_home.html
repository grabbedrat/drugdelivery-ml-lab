Module 3: Drug-Target Interaction and Molecular Modeling

In this module, you will learn about drug-target interactions and molecular modeling techniques. The module covers the following topics:

  • Lesson 1: Molecular Docking and Scoring Functions
  • Lesson 2: Molecular Dynamics Simulations
  • Lesson 3: Machine Learning for Interaction Prediction
  • Case Study: Identifying Potential Drug-Target Interactions

Learning Objectives

By the end of this module, you will be able to:

  • Understand and apply molecular docking and scoring functions.
  • Understand and perform molecular dynamics simulations.
  • Apply machine learning techniques for drug-target interaction prediction.
  • Analyze a case study on identifying potential drug-target interactions.
